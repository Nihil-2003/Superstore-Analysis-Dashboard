# 1. Project Introduction
The Superstore Sales Dashboard is a Excel project designed to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer segments, and product categories across different regions and years.

This dashboard transforms raw retail sales data into interactive visual insights that help businesses track performance, identify profitable areas, and make data-driven decisions.

---

# 2. Project Objective
The main goals of this project are:

- To analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- To monitor monthly sales trends  
- To identify top-performing product categories  
- To compare customer segments and regional sales  
- To create an interactive business intelligence dashboard

# 5. Dashboard Visualizations

## 5.1 Monthly Sales Analysis
Displays monthly sales performance across the year.

## 5.2 Sales by Category
Shows sales generated by product categories such as:
- Technology
- Furniture
- Office Supplies

## 5.3 Customer Segment Analysis
Analyzes sales contribution from:
- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

## 5.4 Regional Sales Analysis
Compares sales performance across regions:
- Central
- East
- South
- West

## 5.5 Sales Trend by Year
Displays yearly sales growth from 2011 to 2014.

## 5.6 Shipping Mode Analysis
Shows distribution of orders by shipping modes:
- First Class
- Second Class
- Standard Class
- Same Day
